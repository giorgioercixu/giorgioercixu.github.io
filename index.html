Biography

Erci Xu is a postdoc of computer science at the Leopard Lab, NUDT. His research interests include distributed computing, storage systems and software/hardware reliability. Besides my research, I also write some book reviews and play some guitar.

My Chinese name is 徐尔茨 (sounds like hsu ertz). I also go by the name of Giorgio, much easier to pronounce now, innit?
