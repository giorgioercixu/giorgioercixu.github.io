Short Bio

Erci Xu is a tenure-track associate professor @ Shanghai Jiao Tong University who mostly focuses on cloud, storage systems, reliability and infrastructure for AI. He has published multiple papers at top venues including SOSP, OSDI, FAST, ATC and Eurosys. Most (conferrence only) of his publications are listed here. He is the recipient of two USENIX FAST Best Paper Awards FAST'23, FAST'24, Distinguished Artifacts Award from FAST'25 and ACM SIGOPS China Rising Star Award. In his spare time, Erci enjoys reading and reviewing books on Goodreads. Some of the reviews (mostly in Chinese) are also posted here.
